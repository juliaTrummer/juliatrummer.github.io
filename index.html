ABOUT ME


Hey there! I'm Julia Trummer, a 25-year-old information design student with a passion for creating intuitive user experiences. My journey into the world of UI and UX design has been an exciting one, and I'm thrilled to share it with you.

Before diving into the world of information design, I spent several years as a software developer. During this time, I gained invaluable insights into the importance of user experience. I witnessed firsthand how a well-designed interface could enhance productivity, streamline processes, and ultimately improve the lives of users.

My transition from software development to UX design was driven by a desire to leverage my technical background to create designs that not only look great but also function seamlessly.
