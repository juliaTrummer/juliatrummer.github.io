Workflow Improvement

Simplified working process

Project Scope:
 As a student studying UI and UX design, I have been working in a small group on a project to improve the booking process and overall organization at a local cycling service shop. The main problem we identified was the need to streamline the booking process for customers and enhance the work management system for employees. Here's how we approached this project:

Understanding the Problem:
 To kick off the project, we began by thoroughly understanding the existing booking process and how employees manage their work. We interviewed the shop's staff to gather insights into their pain points and needs.
Kanban Board Implementation:
 To address the organization and work management issue, we decided to implement a Kanban board system. We chose Kanban because it's easy to track, visually intuitive, and provides real-time updates on tasks and their statuses. We began by researching Kanban best practices to ensure a smooth transition.

Researching Pros and Cons:
 We conducted research to understand the advantages and disadvantages of using Kanban boards in a service shop setting. Pros included improved task visibility, better task prioritization, and enhanced collaboration. Cons included a learning curve and potential resistance to change from employees.

Teamwork in Figma:
 Figma was our primary design and collaboration tool. We worked together in real-time to create wireframes, prototypes, and design concepts. Collaborating in Figma allowed us to iteratively refine our designs based on feedback from each other.
