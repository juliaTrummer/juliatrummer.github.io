Project Scope:
 The UI/UX project involves designing a password management app that simplifies and organizes not only an individual's passwords but also those of their family members. The key problem addressed is the need for a tech-savvy family member to constantly assist parents or grandparents in managing their digital accounts. The primary objectives and features of this app include:

Alleviating Tech Support Burden:
 Many households have a designated tech-savvy family member who often plays the role of IT support. They help others log in, reset passwords, and troubleshoot various digital issues. This app aims to reduce this common tech support burden by providing an easy-to-use platform for managing passwords. Family members no longer need to remember and maintain each other's credentials, reducing the frequency of tech support calls or in-person assistance.

Improving Password Organization:
 Families often have multiple online accounts for various services, and managing these can be a daunting task.
