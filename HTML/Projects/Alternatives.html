Alternative Screens

Educational Game

Problem Statement:
In today's digital age, our constant exposure to screens has become a pressing concern. The Nintendo Switch, a beloved gaming console in countless households, is no exception. It's a source of fun and entertainment, but what if we could leverage its widespread popularity to address a global issue? The problem we address here is the alternative screen design, one that promotes positive change and supports the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

Solution:
The solution lies in the power of education and entertainment, and it starts right at the Nintendo Switch's user interface. By incorporating an alternative screen design that aligns with the SDGs, we can create a world where children and gamers of all ages become informed and inspired to make a difference.
Show Children the Problem:
 Imagine a game where players can explore virtual worlds and ecosystems, but with a twist - they experience the consequences of pollution firsthand. This interactive experience allows us to show children the problem in a compelling and engaging way, fostering empathy and understanding.

Tell Them About the Problem:
 Within the Nintendo Switch UI, we integrate educational components that provide information about the real-world issues surrounding pollution, climate change, and other global challenges. Players can access informative videos, quizzes, and infographics, making learning a seamless part of their gaming experience.
Educate and Have Fun:
 The key is to strike a balance between education and entertainment. Gamers have fun while simultaneously gaining insights into how their choices in the virtual world can impact the real one. By aligning these experiences with the SDGs, players are empowered to make positive changes, both in the game and in their daily lives.

Leveraging Widespread Console Usage:
 The beauty of this solution is that many households already have a Nintendo Switch, making it a readily available platform for spreading awareness and fostering a sense of responsibility. Through our alternative screen design, we can inspire a generation to actively contribute to the global efforts to achieve the SDGs.
