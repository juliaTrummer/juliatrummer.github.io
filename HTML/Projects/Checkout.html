Checkout Screen

Simplified purchasing process

Project Scope:
 For our next project, I undertook an exercise focused on optimizing checkout screens. The objective was to design a simplified shopping cart and a streamlined checkout process. While the complete checkout system comprises various components, this exercise emphasized the most critical elements.

Research and Methodology:
 To accomplish this task, I conducted comprehensive research on established strategies and best practices in the field of e-commerce checkout systems. In particular, I focused on the implementation of progress indicators, order overview, and confirmation and feedback components to enhance the overall user experience.
